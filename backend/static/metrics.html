Understanding Scientific Metrics

Accuracy

Total percentage of correct predictions (Genuine vs AI-Generated).

Precision

The ability to avoid "False Alarms"—not flagging real media as AI.

Recall

The ability to find ALL AI-generated content (minimizing missed detections).

F1-Score

The harmonic mean of Precision and Recall, providing a definitive quality score.
